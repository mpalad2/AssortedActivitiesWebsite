How to Play

You can choose to match 8, 10, or 12 pairs.
The time and difficulty increases when you choose to play with more and more pairs.
When you start the game, you will have a few seconds to memorize the areas the cards are in before the images disappear.
Matched pairs will remain on screen.
If the current cards aren't a match, please allow them to disappear before you select another pair.
Match all cards with their pairs before the time limit ends to win!

Have fun and good luck!!
